Submission Guidelines

Papers must follow CEUR formatting guidelines using the template available at: Overleaf CEUR Template .

We accept submissions in two formats:

  • Extended abstracts: (up to 2 pages + 1 page references)
  • Full papers: (up to 12 pages + 2 pages references)

Submissions may present original, unpublished research, ongoing work, or previously published mature work formatted as a survey or position paper.
